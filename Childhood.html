Childhood

As a child I was very active and wild, I would say being that way it gave me a lot of opportunities to learn so much from life, I learned the key values of life for example living with people in harmony and respecting the elderly. During my childhood my family traveled a lot thus I met quite a number of people also teaching that there's all types of people in this world and everyone has different ways of handling situations. My family tree is quite broad, on majority of the holidays we'd always have a small shindig just to appreciate what we have.
